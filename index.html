Shoppy Cat Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Shoppy Cat ("the app") is a shopping, pantry, and meal planning application designed for individuals and families. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name. If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account name and email address.

App Content

Shopping lists, pantry items, recipes, meal plans, and household data you create are stored securely using Google Firebase to enable synchronization across your devices and sharing with household members.

Camera Access

The app requests camera permission solely for scanning product barcodes. No images or video are stored or transmitted by the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve app functionality
  • To synchronize data across your devices
  • To enable sharing features with users you explicitly invite
  • To process subscription payments through Google Play Billing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for advertising.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (data storage and authentication)
    https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Play Billing (subscription payments)
    https://payments.google.com/payments/apis-secure/get_legal_document?ldo=0&ldt=privacynotice

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Data Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your data. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data by contacting us.
