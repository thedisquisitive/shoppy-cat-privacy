Privacy Policy

Introduction

Thank you for using Shoppy Cat. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data. By using the app you agree to the practices described here.

This policy was last updated on April 14, 2026.

Information We Collect

Shoppy Cat is a personal shopping-list app. It collects only the minimum data needed to provide its features:

  • Shopping list data – items, quantities, categories, and notes you enter. This data is stored locally on your device and, if you enable sync, in your personal cloud account.
  • Device information – basic device identifiers and operating-system version used for crash reporting and compatibility purposes only.
  • Usage analytics – anonymous, aggregated statistics (e.g., feature usage counts) to help us improve the app. This data cannot identify you personally.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, payment details, or any other personally identifiable information unless you explicitly provide it (for example, when contacting support).

How We Use Your Information

We use the data described above solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the core functionality of Shoppy Cat.
  • Diagnose and fix crashes and technical issues.
  • Understand how features are used so we can improve the app.
  • Respond to support requests you initiate.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Your shopping list data is stored locally on your device. If cloud sync is enabled, data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored using industry-standard encryption. We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to keep your device software up to date.

Third-Party Services

Shoppy Cat may integrate with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services – used for app distribution and in-app billing. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase / Crashlytics – used for anonymous crash reporting and analytics. See Firebase's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Shoppy Cat is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access or delete your shopping list data at any time from within the app.
  • Opt out of anonymous analytics in the app settings.
  • Request deletion of any data we hold by contacting us (see below).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "last updated" date at the top of the page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
